WHY DHANUSH SCIENCE ?

PASSIONATE ELECTRONICS ENGINEER

I go by the name Dhanush Science, it's because my love for science and technology has been a part of me since I was a child. I've always been drawn to the wonders of science, and I want to spend my life contributing to it through research and development. It's not just a passion; it's a calling that feels deeply ingrained in my very being.

My Vision

Spark Tech embodies my vision as an organization dedicated to advancing space robotics and addressing real-world challenges through research and innovation. Through continuous learning and hands-on projects, I aim to contribute meaningful solutions to the field of space exploration and robotics.

My Journey

Currently pursuing my bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering at Vidyavardhaka College of Engineering, Mysore, I am dedicated to bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical applications. My projects, including the SPECTRA rocket motor test stand, EAGLE model rocket, and PHOENIX avionics board, represent my commitment to hands-on learning and innovation.

Future Goals

My clear goal is to become a space robotics scientist, contributing to the advancement of space exploration technologies. I believe that every challenge is an opportunity to learn and grow, and I'm committed to continuous self-improvement in pursuit of this dream.
